ReminderRides

App integration of iOS calendars and Uber™.

Designate a location, choose a time, make a name for your event, right in your calendar app. We parse the location and time, and send you a reminder to book an Uber™ when the time comes. Specify a location when you get that party invite and forget about it later!

Amazing Features
